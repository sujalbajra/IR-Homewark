Documents and queries are represented as vectors in a multi-dimensional space.

  • TF-IDF Weighting: Assigns weights to terms based on frequency.
  • Cosine Similarity: Measures the angle between the query vector and document vector.
  • Ranking: Documents are ranked by their similarity score (0 to 1).
